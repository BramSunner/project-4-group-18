Our Dataset

We combined two datasets, one from Kaggle and one from the TMDB API. The dataset comprised several features. For the recommender, we focused on overview, director, cast, genre.

Our Inspiration

Who doesn't love movies? Traditional streaming sites recommendation bars are too complicated and verbose. No one has the time or energy to to browse 100 titles.

Navigating The Site

Welcome to our movie recommender website! This is for people who want to find movies similar to the ones they already know and love.

Go to "Movie Recommender", input a movie, set the number of recommendations you want, and voila - it's showtime!
